---
title: "Making a Payment Using ClickPesa BillPay"
description: "Learn how to make payments using ClickPesa BillPay through various mobile money services and banking options in Tanzania."
---

Customers can conveniently make payments using ClickPesa **BillPay Control Number** through Tanzania’s leading mobile money services such as Tigopesa, Airtel Money, Halopesa and M-Pesa, as well as CRDB SIM Banking (APP and USSD), CRDB Internet Banking and CRDB Wakalas.



### [Pay via Airtel Money](#pay-via-airtel-money)
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Airtel Money USSD">
1. Piga `*150*60#`
2. Chagua Chaguo `5` --- **Lipia Bili**
3. Chagua Chaguo `2` --- **Chagua Kampuni**
4. Chagua Chaguo `7` --- **Chagua Kampuni**
5. Weka Namba --- **47**
6. Ingiza namba ya Kumbukumbu --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
7. Weka Kiasi
8. Chagua `1` --- **(Ndio) Kuthibitisha**
9. Weka PIN
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Airtel Money APP">
1. Fungua AirtelMoney App
2. Bonyeza **Lipa Bili**
3. Bonyeza **Ingiza jina la biashara au namba**
4. Andika *"clickpesa"*
5. Chagua **CLICKPESA TANZANIA**
6. Weka Kumbukumbu ya Malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
7. Weka Kiasi
8. Bonyeza **Endelea**
9. Weka Namba ya Siri
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### [Pay via M-Pesa](#pay-via-m-pesa)
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="M-Pesa USSD">
1. Piga `*150*01#`
2. Chagua Chaguo `4` --- **Lipia Bili**
3. Chagua Chaguo `3` --- **Ingiza Namba ya Kampuni**
4. Ingiza Namba ya Kampuni --- **[M-Pesa Merchant Number]** - *Available at onboarding*
5. Weka Kumbukumbu ya Malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
6. Ingiza Kiasi
7. Ingiza Namba ya Siri
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="M-Pesa App">
1. Fungua M-Pesa App
2. Bonyeza **Hamisha Pesa**
3. Bonyeza **Lipia kwa M-Pesa**
4. Bonyeza **Kitambulisho cha biashara** na Weka **[M-Pesa Merchant Number]**
5. Bonyeza **Kumbukumbu** na Weka **[BillPay Control Number]**
6. Weka Kiasi
8. Bonyeza **Endelea**
9. Bonyeza **Lipa sasa** na Ingiza Namba ya Siri
  </Tab>
</Tabs>


### [Pay via Mixx by Yas (Tigopesa/Zantel)](#pay-via-mixx-by-yas-tigopesa%2Fzantel)
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Mixx USSD">
1. Piga `*150*01#`
2. Chagua Chaguo `4` --- **Lipia Bili**
3. Chagua Chaguo `3` --- **Ingiza Namba ya Kampuni**
4. Ingiza Namba ya Kampuni --- **889999**
5. Weka Kumbukumbu ya Malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
6. Ingiza Kiasi
7. Ingiza Namba ya Siri
  </Tab>
</Tabs>


### [Pay via CRDB](#pay-via-crdb)
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="SimBanking USSD">
1. Piga `*150*03#`
2. Chagua `1` --- **Simbanking**
3. Weka Namba ya Siri
4. Chagua `4` --- **Pay Bills**
5. Chagua `6`--- **Taasisi**
6. Chagua `7` --- **Nyinginezo**
7. Chagua `N` –-- **Next**
8. Chagua `N` –-- **Next**
9. Chagua **CLICKPESA**
10. Weka Namba ya Malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
11. Ingiza Kiasi
12. Thibitisha Malipo
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="SimBanking APP">
1. Fungua CRDB SimBanking App
2. Weka Namba ya Siri ya SimBanking
3. Bonyeza **Malipo** katika menyu ya chini
4. Bonyeza **Malipo Zaidi**
5. Chagua **CLICKPESA** Kwenye listi au andika **CLICKPESA** Juu Kwenye Tafuta huduma
6. Weka Namba ya Malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
7. Bonyeza **PATA TAARIFA**
8. Hakikisha Taarifa za Bili, Chagua Akaunti Ya Kutoa na Weka Kiasi
9. Bonyeza **ENDELEA**
10. Hakikisha Taarifa za Muamala na Bonyeza **THIBITISHA**
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CRDB Internet Banking">
1. Login into your CRDB Internet Banking portal
2. Select **Payment**
3. Select **Bill Payment**
4. Company Category --- Select **Others**
5. Company Name -- Select **CLICKPESA**
6. Enter Reference Number --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CRDB Wakala (App/POS)">
1. Tembelea wakala CRDB aliye karibu nawe
2. Omba kulipia bili kwa namba ya malipo ya CRDB
3. Toa jina la taasisi --- **CLICKPESA**
4. Toa namba ya nmaba ya malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
5. Toa kiasi
  </Tab>

    <Tab title="CRDB Branch (OTC)">
1. Tembelea tawi la CRDB lililo karibu nawe
2. Omba kulipia bili kwa namba ya malipo ya CRDB
3. Toa jina la taasisi --- **CLICKPESA**
4. Toa namba ya nmaba ya malipo --- **[BillPay Control Number]**
5. Toa kiasi
  </Tab>

</Tabs>
