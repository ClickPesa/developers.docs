---
title: "Checkout Overview"
description: "ClickPesa Checkout is a ready-to-use payment acceptance experience provided by ClickPesa."
---

It enables seamless payment collection and supports a full range of payment methods and currencies with minimal integration.

ClickPesa Checkout is available as part of ClickPesa products like [E-invoice](https://merchant.clickpesa.com/invoices) and [Payment Pages](https://merchant.clickpesa.com/payments), where it comes built-in, but it is also [available for integration](/checkout/hosted-checkout/hosted-checkout) into your web application or platform for a seamless payment experience.
